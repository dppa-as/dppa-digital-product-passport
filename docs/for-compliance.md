## What DPP Means for Compliance Teams

As a compliance leader, you are at the frontline of meeting evolving EU and EEA sustainability regulations. Digital Product Passports (DPPs) represent a fundamental shift in how product data is documented, maintained, and shared ‚Äî from manufacturing through end-of-life.

Whether you manage compliance for electronics, batteries, textiles, or complex consumer goods, you need a solution that makes regulatory adherence straightforward, auditable, and future-proof.
